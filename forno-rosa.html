The oven does the talking

Since 2016

A single wood-fired oven, built by hand, never turned off.

Forno Rosa started as a wood oven in a courtyard and a short list of things worth cooking in it. We still keep it that way: a tight menu, dough proofed for a full day, and a fire that's been burning since the doors opened.

No shortcuts, no reheats. If the oven's not ready, the kitchen's not open — that's the whole philosophy, and it hasn't changed.
